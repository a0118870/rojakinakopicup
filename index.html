Rojak in a Kopi Cup

The snack dish rojak is a mixture of various ingredients such as taupok, youtiao and a host of other vegetables. However, 'rojak' is sometimes used to describe Singapore culture -- a mixture of various cultural components from other cultures. Rojak in a Kopi Cup thus uses rojak as a means to understand Singapore's culture and what that implies and what implications it has for Singapore.
